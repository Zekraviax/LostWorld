Key Word: Disable
Disabling an equipped item prevents any cards it gives from being played, prevents any passive, 
triggered, or active effects from being used, and prevents the equipped item from giving any 
stats.


Key Word: Destroy
Any card that is Destroyed is removed from the entitys' Deck, Collection, and all battle zones
(Draw pile, Discard pile, Void). Any items that are destroyed cannot be used, are removed from
the inventory, are unequipped if they were equipped, and any cards that they give are also 
destroyed.
